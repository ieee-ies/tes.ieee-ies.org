TC on Technology Ethics and Society

Welcome to the IEEE IES Technical Committee on Technology Ethics and Society (TC-TES) website!

This committee aims to provide a forum for researchers and practitioners to exchange their latest achievements and to identify critical issues and challenges for future investigation on Machine Ethics, Artificial Intelligence, Technology Ethical Impact, Societal Implications etc. Check the details on the different areas in scope of this TC.

In this web site you can find information about the activities undertaken by the Technical Committee. If you wish to be involved in these activities, and be kept up to date with the community, join the TC-TES.

Please fill in the TES survey and influence the future of the TES TC!
